My Coding Journey

I officially started my coding journey on June 5th, 2021. I had started, and stopped, twice before but this time I was really motivated! I was ready to change my career and learn a new skill. I primarily used Codecademy, but I also used YouTube to help me understand the more complex concepts. To help me stay focused and accountable I set a goal and kept a daily journal of my progress. My goal was to get to 100% completion in the Full-Stack Developer course on Codecademy in 100 days (excluding Tuesdays which was the only night I took off every week).

I’ve included that journal here!
